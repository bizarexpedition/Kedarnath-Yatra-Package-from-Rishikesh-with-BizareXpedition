<h2>Kedarnath Yatra from Rishikesh – Route & Experience</h2>
The Kedarnath yatra from Rishikesh is one of the most spiritually enriching journeys in Uttarakhand. The route typically follows:<br>
<b>Rishikesh → Devprayag → Srinagar → Rudraprayag → Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund → Kedarnath</b><br>
<b>Devprayag:</b> Confluence of Alaknanda & Bhagirathi rivers forming the Ganga<br>
<b>Rudraprayag:</b> Sacred meeting point of Alaknanda & Mandakini<br>
<b>Guptkashi:</b> Important stop for rest and temple visits<br>
<b>Gaurikund:</b> Base point for Kedarnath trek<br>

From Gaurikund, pilgrims can reach Kedarnath Temple via:<br>
Trek (16–18 km)<br>
Pony or palki services<br>
Helicopter services (from nearby helipads like Phata/Sersi)<br>
This journey blends spiritual devotion, scenic Himalayan landscapes, and cultural richness.
