<h2>Kedarnath Yatra from Rishikesh – Route & Experience</h2>
The Kedarnath yatra from Rishikesh is one of the most spiritually enriching journeys in Uttarakhand. The route typically follows:
<b>Rishikesh → Devprayag → Srinagar → Rudraprayag → Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund → Kedarnath</b>
Devprayag: Confluence of Alaknanda & Bhagirathi rivers forming the Ganga
Rudraprayag: Sacred meeting point of Alaknanda & Mandakini
Guptkashi: Important stop for rest and temple visits
Gaurikund: Base point for Kedarnath trek
From Gaurikund, pilgrims can reach Kedarnath Temple via:
Trek (16–18 km)
Pony or palki services
Helicopter services (from nearby helipads like Phata/Sersi)
This journey blends spiritual devotion, scenic Himalayan landscapes, and cultural richness.
